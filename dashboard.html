AI-Driven Discovery of High-Affinity Ligands for Developing Bio-inspired ECMO Interfaces Capable of Immune Phenotypic Reprogramming

人工智能驱动的高亲和力配体筛选及其介导的ECMO仿生界面免疫重塑研究

This research environment combines structured ligand ranking, autonomous literature monitoring, and a live reasoning assistant to help the team identify ECMO-surface ligands that are not only high-affinity, but also experimentally actionable and biologically suitable.

Scientific objective Prioritize candidates that can engage inhibitory pathways while remaining compatible with ECMO surface engineering.
Operational workflow Rank candidates, inspect structure, interrogate tradeoffs with the assistant, then refine wet-lab next steps.
